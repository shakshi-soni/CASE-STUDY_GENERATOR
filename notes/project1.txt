client: small farmer cooperative, rural Maharashtra region
problem: farmers struggling to access mandi prices, weather updates, crop disease treatment info, and government scheme details - scattered across different sources, 
often outdated, many farmers don't have smartphones with apps
what we built: a WhatsApp-based agriculture bot - farmers message it and get mandi 
prices, weather forecast, crop disease/treatment suggestions, and govt scheme alerts
took about 2-3 weeks to build
piloted with around 35-40 farmers across two villages
farmers said it saved them time they used to spend traveling to check mandi prices, 
no exact number tracked on time saved
some farmers reported catching a crop disease early because of the bot's alerts, 
cooperative head mentioned this anecdotally, not formally logged
adoption was slower with older farmers, faster with younger/literate ones
