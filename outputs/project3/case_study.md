# Case Study: Redesigning Onboarding for a Fintech Startup
## Problem
A small pre-seed fintech startup with a 5-person team was experiencing high drop-off rates in their onboarding flow, but the exact reasons were unclear.
## Approach
To address this issue, a redesigned onboarding process was implemented, accompanied by the addition of analytics tracking to identify specific drop points.
## Solution
The solution involved a 4-week project timeline, focusing on streamlining the onboarding experience and integrating analytics for data-driven insights.
## Results
The results showed an improvement in drop-off rates, as reported by the founder. Additionally, qualitative feedback indicated that support tickets related to 'confusing signup' virtually disappeared. The founder expressed high satisfaction, stating it was the best money they spent that year.
