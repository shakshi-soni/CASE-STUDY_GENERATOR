# Case Study: Travel Planning Bot
## Problem
A small independent travel agency faced a challenge where clients often overpaid for flights and trains due to rapid price changes and the time-consuming process of manual checks across multiple sites. Additionally, itinerary planning was a labor-intensive task that took agents several hours per client.
## Approach
To address this issue, a travel planning bot was developed. This bot checks live flight and train prices, constructs an itinerary within the client's specified budget, and considers the weather at the destination.
## Solution
The travel planning bot was built over the course of 2-3 weeks.
## Results
The agency reported that the planning time per client 'dropped a lot' after implementing the bot, although an exact number of hours saved was not provided. There was also a decrease in the number of itinerary revisions per client, but the agency did not share the specific before and after counts. Some clients noted that they were able to book cheaper flights than expected, but the agency did not formally track total savings across all clients.
